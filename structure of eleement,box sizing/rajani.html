Rajani Kanth

His bir./rajani.pngth name is Shivaji Rao Gaekwad, but the silver screen gave him the name Rajinikanth. He was born on 12 December 1950. He had learned the Marathi and Kannada, both languages , in his childhood. He is a south actor. He studied at Gavipuram Government Kannada Model Primary school. After that, his admission was done in the centre of Ramakrishna Mission. He learned about Indian culture in that centre and participated in the drama also. After taking part in the play Mahabharata, he decided that he has to make his career in acting. He played the role of Ekalavya's friend in that drama. He changed his school and took admission to Acharya Pathshala Public school.
The financial condition of his family was bad. Therefore, along with his studies, he was also working as a carpenter, porter and he also worked as a bus conductor in the Bangalore bus services. He also applied for the job, but he didn't get the job. He took admission to the Madras film Institute for learning acting. He also learned Tamil for working in Tamil movies. His first film was Katha Sangama. He acted in the Apporva Raagangal movie in 1975 that was a Tamil movie. He started to become famous, and In the 90s, he was the biggest commercial entertainer. He worked in Hindi movies. He performed role of the romantic hero, action hero, and comedy hero. His superhit films are Robot, Chandramukhi and Sivaji, etc. He has worked with many actresses like Sowcar Janaki, Shobana, Sripriya, Manorama, Raadhika Sarathkumar, Sridevi, Gautami, Seetha, Meena, Rambha and Soundarya etc.
